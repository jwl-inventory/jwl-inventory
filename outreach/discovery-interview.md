# Discovery Interview Guide — Phase 3

Goal: 10 calls, identify the top 3 non-negotiable features.

## Before the call
- Review the atelier's Instagram / website
- Note what types of pieces they make (rings, necklaces, bespoke commissions)
- Note their apparent size (solo goldsmith vs. team)

## Opening (2 min)
"Thank you so much for your time. I have about 20 minutes of questions — mostly me listening. There are no wrong answers; I'm trying to understand your real workflow, not sell anything today."

## Core questions

### Current tools
1. Walk me through how you currently track your pieces. What do you use?
2. When you get a custom commission, how do you manage it from inquiry to delivery?
3. How do you keep track of the gemstones and materials you have in stock?
4. How do you calculate price for a custom piece?

### Pain points
5. What's the most frustrating part of your current system?
6. Have you ever lost track of a piece, a stone, or a client detail? What happened?
7. What do you do when a client asks where their order is?

### Workflow specifics
8. How many pieces are "in flight" at any given time?
9. Do you work with a team, or is it just you managing everything?
10. Do you use any photos to document pieces? Where do those live?

### Willingness to pay
11. If a tool solved [their biggest pain point], what would that be worth to you per month?
12. Have you ever paid for software to help run your business? What did you use? Did it stick?

## Closing
"Based on what you've shared, it sounds like [X] is the biggest challenge. That's exactly what I'm trying to solve. Can I add you to the beta list and follow up when we have something to show you?"
